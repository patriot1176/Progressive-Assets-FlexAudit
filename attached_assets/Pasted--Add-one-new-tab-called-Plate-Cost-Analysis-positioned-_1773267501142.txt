SECTION 1 — JOB & PLATE CONFIGURATION

Number of New Jobs per Year (default 0)
Avg Colors per Job (default 0)
Avg Plate Cost per Color ($) (default 0)
Avg Selling Price ($/ft) (default 0.21)
Avg Run Length per Job (ft) (default 0)

SECTION 2 — COPY CHANGE EVENTS

Number of Copy Change Events per Year (default 0)
Avg Plates Changed per Copy Change (default 1)
Avg Plate Cost per Copy Change Plate ($) (default 0)

OUTPUT SECTION 1 — ANNUAL PLATE COST SUMMARY

New Job Plate Cost per Job: (Avg Colors × Avg Plate Cost per Color) | $0
Copy Change Plate Cost per Event: (Avg Plates Changed × Avg Plate Cost per Copy Change Plate) | $0
Total Annual New Job Plate Cost: (New Jobs × Colors × Plate Cost) | $0
Total Annual Copy Change Plate Cost: (Copy Change Events × Plates Changed × Plate Cost) | $0
TOTAL ANNUAL PLATE COST: sum of both | $0
V12 Annual Savings: total annual plate cost | Eliminated
Color code the V12 column green. Color code the Flexo column red for any value over $10,000.

OUTPUT SECTION 2 — RUN LENGTH BREAK-EVEN ANALYSIS
Show a table with the header: 'At what run length does a job cover its plate cost?'

Break-Even Footage = (Avg Colors × Avg Plate Cost per Color) ÷ Avg Selling Price ($/ft)
Display this number prominently as: 'This job must run at least X feet just to cover plate cost'
Below it show a comparison table for these run lengths: 500 ft, 1,000 ft, 2,500 ft, 5,000 ft, 10,000 ft, and one custom input row:

Columns: Run Length | Job Revenue | Plate Cost | Setup Covers Revenue? | Net After Plates
Job Revenue = Run Length × Avg Selling Price
Plate Cost = Avg Colors × Avg Plate Cost per Color (flat per job regardless of length)
Net After Plates = Job Revenue minus Plate Cost
Color code each row: red if Net After Plates is negative, yellow if positive but less than 20% margin, green if positive and above 20% margin



OUTPUT SECTION 3 — SKU FAMILY ANALYSIS
Header: 'What does a high-SKU job family cost in plates?'

One input field: Number of SKUs in Job Family (default 0)
Display:

Total Plate Cost for SKU Family (Flexo): SKUs × Colors × Plate Cost per Color
Total Plate Cost for SKU Family (V12): $0
If every SKU gets one copy change per year: SKUs × Plates Changed per Copy Change × Plate Cost per Copy Change Plate
Combined Annual Plate Spend on this Job Family (Flexo): sum of both
Combined Annual Plate Spend on this Job Family (V12): $0
Prominent callout box showing the difference labeled: 'Annual Plate Savings by Moving This Job Family to V12'
